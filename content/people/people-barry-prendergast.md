Product & Service Designer based in Germany, specializing in design strategy, systems, and DesignOps. Creator of <a href="https://www.lanyards.app/" target="_blank">Lanyards.app</a>—a curated profile platform for academic researchers built on ATProto.

---

## Current Work

**Creator of <a href="https://www.lanyards.app/" target="_blank">Lanyards.app</a>** (beta) - A curated profile platform for academic researchers that aggregates scholarly presence in one location. Built on AT Protocol, Lanyards consolidates publications, talks, affiliations, and social profiles with zero maintenance overhead through auto-ingestion from ORCID and Google Scholar.

**Design Leader** - Focuses on design strategy, practice, and systems. Uses design to ease startup growing pains and advocates for design metrics aligned with business outcomes.
