Information architect, innovation strategist, and founder of <a href="https://myhub.ai/@mathewlowry/" target="_blank">MyHub.ai</a>, exploring AI4Communities and decentralized collective intelligence on ATProto. Based in Brussels with 30 years pioneering digital transformation for European institutions.

---

## Current Work

**Founder of <a href="https://myhub.ai/@mathewlowry/" target="_blank">MyHub.ai</a>** (alpha) - A content curation and creation platform for people who care about content, exploring what happens when you add AI4communities apps into the ATProto ecosystem.

**Innovation & Content Strategist** - Information architect for the European Commission's Knowledge4Policy, architecting "one of the most powerful knowledge management, synthesis and community platforms on EUROPA."

**ATProto Explorer** - Actively exploring the ATProto ecosystem, particularly through platforms like WhiteWind. Believes this represents the start of a profound longterm shift towards decentralized collective intelligence.

## Professional Background

**30 Years in Brussels** - Since 1991, developed communication and digital transformation strategies for EU Institutions, venture capital firms, research projects, and media startups.

**Digital Pioneer:** First database-driven website by a European public organization (1995), first Web2.0 online community for European Commission (2002), most successful EU policy blogging platform (2007)

**Science Communication Background** - Theoretical physics degree from Australia, discovered first online community on Usenet in 1988, began career as science journalist explaining complex issues to non-specialists

## Expertise & Community

**Expertise:** Information architecture, communication strategy, digital transformation, community management, content strategy, knowledge management
